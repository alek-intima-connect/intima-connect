Key Features of Intima Connect

Gamified Sex Education

Interactive lessons, quizzes, and streak rewards that teach concepts like consent, contraception, healthy communication, and anatomy in a way that’s fun and memorable.

Story-Based Learning

Choose-your-own-adventure style storytelling that presents real-life situations and teaches through emotionally engaging choices and outcomes.

Science-Backed Content

Every lesson and answer is reviewed by certified educators, sexual health professionals, and psychologists to ensure accuracy and inclusivity.

Personal Progress Tracking

Users earn badges, track progress across modules, and unlock premium challenges, all while maintaining full data privacy.

Integrated Wellness Coaching

Optional one-on-one chat-based or video coaching with licensed sex educators and relationship therapists, available via in-app purchase or subscription.

Inclusive for All Identities

Content tailored for LGBTQ+, non-binary, culturally diverse users with personalized learning paths and safe topic filters.
